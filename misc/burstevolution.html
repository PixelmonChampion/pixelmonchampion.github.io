Burst Evolution

Burst Evolution originates from Ultra Space, where burst crystals rained down upon the Cento region 200 years ago, causing widespread devastation. These crystals, infused with burst energy, allow certain Pokémon to undergo a transformation when equipped with a burst crystal. The burst evolution enhances the Pokémon’s abilities, modifies its stats, and adds three unique moves to its movepool. To trigger a burst evolution, simply equip a burst crystal on your Pokémon and send it into battle; the Pokémon will automatically burst evolve when thrown out or during battle initiation. Removing the burst crystal clears the added moves from the Pokémon’s movepool, making the process temporary. The origins of burst energy remain a mystery, with some believing it came from Ultra Space or from the effect of an ancient Pokémon. The burst crystals are both revered and feared by the people of Cento, who are both cautious and curious about their potential.
