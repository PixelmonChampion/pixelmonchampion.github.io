Frequently Asked Questions

What is PokéFrontiers?

PokéFrontiers is a Cobblemon server that's been years in the making, designed to offer a unique and outstanding Pokémon experience. It features custom-made Fakemon with their own models and textures, set in the custom-built Monsai Frontier, which includes its own story, ranking sysyem, and unique gameplay mechanics. The server blends a mmo-survival experience with an adventure experience, providing handcrafted custom terrain, rich lore, dungeons, and loot. PokéFrontiers aims to balance our Pokémon world to encourage both solo and cooperative play, offering a one-of-a-kind Cobblemon adventure.
