Centonian Lopunny

The Glacial Fighter Pokémon

Pokédex Entry

Centonian Lopunny channels the elegance of its original form into raw strength and precision, becoming a guardian of the frozen wilderness. Its legs are reinforced by layers of icy energy, making its kicks powerful enough to shatter icebergs. Its fighting spirit reflects its deep connection to the snowy plains it calls home, where it defends its territory and companions with unyielding determination.
