Champion Oswald

Oswald is the pinnacle of strength and strategy in the Cento region, admired by all Gym Leaders and trainers. A confident and charismatic Champion, he blends humor with a no-nonsense attitude when defending his title. Oswald’s team is a showcase of Cento’s diverse Pokémon, each battle proving their immense potential. Always prepared with a hidden ace, Oswald is a formidable foe who thrives under pressure and takes pride in representing the region.
