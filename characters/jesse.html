Gym Leader Jesse

Jesse is the rugged and experienced Rock-type Gym Leader of Shuca Town. A classic cowboy in the vein of Clint Eastwood, he embodies the spirit of the frontier with his 1v1 battle style. Jesse’s Druddigon stands as a loyal companion and symbol of his strength. Together, they guard their quiet, Western-inspired town and challenge trainers who dare to test their grit.
