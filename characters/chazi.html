Gym Leader Chazi

Chazi is the rebellious, laid-back Poison-type Gym Leader of a coastal town. His carefree attitude often masks his arrogance, and he spends much of his time relaxing with his Galarian Weezing and Centonian Scrafty. Though his gym’s toxic waste pollutes the nearby ocean, Galarian Weezing keeps the town's air clean. Known for his biker-like persona, Chazi brings an edgy, chaotic energy to his battles, challenging trainers with his unpredictable style.
