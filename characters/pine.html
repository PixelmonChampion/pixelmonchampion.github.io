Professor Pine

Prof. Pine is the Cento region's esteemed Pokémon Professor, known for his deep appreciation of the bonds between trainers and Pokémon. He explores these relationships through Cento's rich traditions, blending ancient customs with modern practices. Prof. Pine delights in guiding new trainers on their journeys by gifting them their first partner Pokémon. Renowned for his vast knowledge of regional lore and expert training tips, he is regarded as a master of Pokémon studies and the region’s go-to authority on Cento's history and culture.
