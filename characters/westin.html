Gym Leader Westin

Westin is a passionate Bug Pokémon collector who has scoured the Cento region to assemble a diverse and formidable team. As the Bug-type Gym Leader in a forested town, he thrives in the natural habitat of his Pokémon, honing his skills and strategies. While he doesn’t receive many challengers due to Raylee’s reputation, those who face him discover the cunning and creativity of a true Bug Master. Westin is always ready to ensnare his opponents in an intricate web of tactics.
