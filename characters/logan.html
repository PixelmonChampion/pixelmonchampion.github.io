Rival Logan

Raised by his daycare-owning parents in Tanga Ranch, Logan grew up surrounded by Pokémon, gaining an early understanding of breeding and raising them. His first Pokémon hatched from an egg, sparking a deep bond. Optimistic and persistent, Logan never lets challenges get him down, embodying the spirit of friendly competition. He often visits Chople City when not helping at the daycare, hoping to meet a trainer he can finally call his true rival.
