Gym Leader Edmund

Edmund, the blacksmith of Babiri Town, forges both weapons and his reputation as the Steel-type Gym Leader. Clad in a suit of armor, Edmund is a steadfast defender of his city. His battle style focuses on unyielding defense, with the power to strike back fiercely. Known for his dedication to craftsmanship, Edmund displays his prized Poké Balls and Burst Prisms as a testament to his passion and expertise.
