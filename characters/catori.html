Gym Leader Catori

Catori is a wise elder and mentor hailing from Rindo Village. Deeply connected to her tribe’s traditions, she represents the harmony between people and Pokémon. As the Grass-type Gym Leader, she uses her partner Sunflora to test trainers’ skills and provides guidance for those who fall short. Adorned in traditional attire, Catori is a beacon of wisdom and strength, inspiring those who seek her knowledge.
