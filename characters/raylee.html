Gym Leader Raylee

Once a dedicated member of Kanto's renowned Fighting Dojo, Raylee charted her own path after questioning her old philosophy of overwhelming every opponent. Now the Fighting-type Gym Leader of Fistfight City, she channels her strength into teaching discipline and resilience. Known for her intense battles, Raylee rarely loses, and challengers quickly learn not to take her lightly. Her tenacity inspires the many trainers who follow her, seeking to gain her strength and join the ranks of Fistfight's elite.
